This site is the new foundation for Livo marketing, support and product communication.

<!-- truncate -->

## What is included in the first release

- a focused landing page
- documentation for setup and usage
- a support page for store compliance and user help
- privacy and terms pages
- a blog for feature announcements and release notes

## Why we changed the structure

The previous static HTML approach was workable for a few pages, but it was not sustainable for growing
documentation and multilingual support. Docusaurus gives Livo a better content workflow without adding
backend complexity.
