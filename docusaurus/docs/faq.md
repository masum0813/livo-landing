# FAQ

## Does Livo provide channels?

No. Livo is only a player for sources added by the user.

## Does Livo sell subscriptions?

No. Subscription, stream and channel access are not sold by the app.

## Why is my playlist not working?

The issue may be caused by invalid credentials, provider restrictions, unsupported formats or network problems.
