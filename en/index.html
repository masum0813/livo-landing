📺 What is Livo?

Livo is a personal IPTV player that allows you to play your own M3U and Xtream Codes playlists.

Livo does NOT provide any content, channels, or subscriptions.

⭐ Features

  • Add & manage M3U / Xtream sources
  • Automatic Live / Movies / Series categorization (if supported by playlist)
  • Modern playback interface
  • Resume watching where you left off
  • Light & Dark mode
