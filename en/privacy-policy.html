Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

Livo is a personal IPTV player app that allows users to add and play their own M3U or Xtream Codes playlists. Livo does not offer any content, channels, or IPTV subscriptions.

1. Data We Collect

Livo does not collect personal data directly. However, third-party services may collect anonymized information:

Firebase Analytics

  • App usage metrics
  • Screen views
  • Device model & OS version

Firebase Crashlytics

  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous device identifiers

Google AdMob

  • Ad impressions & clicks
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Personalized ad data (only with user consent)

Livo does not upload or store your IPTV playlists, channels, or URLs.

2. How Data Is Used

  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Diagnose crashes
  • Understand feature usage
  • Optimize ad performance

3. Data Sharing

Livo does not sell or share user data. Third-party services handle anonymized analytics data under their own privacy policies.

4. Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5+, personalized ads require Apple’s App Tracking Transparency permission. Users may deny or change this anytime.

5. IPTV Content Disclaimer

Livo does not own, host, or distribute any IPTV streams. The user is responsible for all content added to the app.

6. Policy Changes

The Privacy Policy may be updated; major changes will be noted on this page.

7. Contact

For support or privacy questions, email livoiptv@gmail.com.
