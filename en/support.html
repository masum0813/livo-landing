About Livo

Livo is a personal IPTV player built for your own M3U and Xtream Codes playlists. The app does not provide channels, subscriptions, or any IPTV content.

Support & Feedback

For bug reports, feature ideas, or general questions, email livoiptv@gmail.com and we will reply as soon as possible.

Quick Notes

  • Livo only plays playlists provided directly by the user.
  • Playlists that rely on Geo-IP restrictions may not work everywhere.
  • Unsupported formats or invalid URLs might not play correctly.
